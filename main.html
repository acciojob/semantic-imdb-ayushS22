Jab We Met (2007)

Actors Quotes

Summary

The film follows Aditya, a heartbroken businessman who meets Geet, a lively and talkative girl, on a train. She forces him to embrace life again as they embark on an unexpected journey. However, when circumstances change, their roles reverse, and Aditya helps Geet rediscover herself.

Actors

  • Shahid Kapoor as Aditya Kashyap
  • Kareena Kapoor as Geet Dhillon
